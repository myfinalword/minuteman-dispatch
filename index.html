What this site is for

A place to read, not just scroll

A daily-updated news wire pulling from sources you choose, a discussion board for organizing locally, and a clearly-labeled space for stories that are circulating but not yet confirmed — so unverified claims never get mixed in with reported news.

Built around three principles

1) Sourced news is labeled by outlet and date. 2) Unverified claims live only in the Allegedly Corner, clearly marked. 3) The War Room is for organizing and debate — not for anonymous pile-ons.
